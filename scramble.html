Ice Scramble

HISTORY

Traditionally, Street vendors already prepare all the ingredients at home then mix them on the spot. The exact origin of Ice Scramble is hard to pinpoint, with some believing it originated in Iloilo.

The big Styrofoam boxes, however, eventually made their way to Manila schoolyards in the ‘70s and ‘80s as a popular pampalamig of children.

Procedure

  • Step 1

    • In a bowl combine shaved ice, powdered milk and sugar. Mix. Add vanilla and red food color. Mixwell.

  • Step 2

    • Fill clear disposable cups with the scramble mixture. Topped with powdered milk, candy sprinkles, rice crispies and mini marshmallows. Then drizzle with condensed milk and chocolate syrup. Serve with straw or plastic spoon.
