Balut

HISTORY

Balut is a traditional food in the Philippines. It was introduced to the Philippines by the Chinese in 1565 or around 1885. Balut has a long and storied history, dating back to pre-colonial times in the Philippines.

Its origins can be traced to Chinese traders who introduced the practice of hatching duck eggs. Over the centuries, balut evolved, incorporating Filipino flavors and culinary techniques. Controversies arose as knowledge of the food spread around the Southeast Asian countries and then globally
